A wryt with in shall vnto you declare •
and in effecte pleynly spesyfye •
wher ye shall ponishe and where as ye shall spare •
and how þat mercy shall rygor modefye •
and your estate also to magnyfye •
this arke of god to make you gracyous •
shall still abyde with you in your hous •
ffor whylles it bydethe still in your presence •
the hygħ lord shall blesse both you and yours •
of grace of fortune send you influence •
and of vertue all þe freshe floures •
and of aduersyte voyd all waye þe shures •
sett pees and reste welfare and vnytie •
durynge youre tyme thorugħe this sytie / lenuoyefinis1 •
Notes
  1. The scribe concluded with "lenvoye, struck it through, and replaced it with "finis." ↩
