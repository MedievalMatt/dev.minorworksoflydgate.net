Quis dabit capiti •
Meo fontem lacrimarium •
Who shal give / vnto myn heed awelle •
Of bittir teerys / my sorwes to compleyne •
Or a gret conduit / of trowbly watis felle •
done to distille / fro myn eyen tweyne •
to shewe the constreynt / of my dedly peyne •
whan I allas beheeld / and did see •
My deere sone / bleede in euery veyne •
Attwix too theevys / nailed to a tree •
