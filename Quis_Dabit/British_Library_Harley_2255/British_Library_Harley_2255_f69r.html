Folio 69 Recto

¶
ffor mannys love / he fauħt a gret batail •
with his sevene hedys / he outraied þe dragoun •
lyk myħty Sampson / withoute plate or mayl •
In his strong fiħt / he stranglid the leoun •
Thus was my sone / mankyndis champioun •
Thoruħ his moost myħty magnanymyte •
as kyng and bisshop / made his oblacioun •
vpon the hiħ auhter / of the roode tre •
¶
My sonys suffraunce / to sathan was gret wrak •
whos gret meeknesse / did I nouħ suffise •
Cleerly figured / whan that Isaak •
Was by his fadir / offrid in sacrifise •
Nat disobeyeng / in no maner wyse •
but like a lamb / of lownesse list nat fle •
But moost myn herte / that tyme did agrise •
Whan I first sauh hym nailed to a tre •
¶
He myhte be callyd Eleasar the secounde •
the champioun / moost myħty and notable •
That gaf tholifaunt / his last mortal wounde •
Machabeorum / this stoory is no fable •
And as hercules / in his conquestys stable •
Bar vp the heuenys / in his humanyte •
ffor whom my sorwys / wer maad moost lamentable •
whan I beheeld hym / thus nailed to a tre •
