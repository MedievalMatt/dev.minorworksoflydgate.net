Thorougħ hir migħty excellence •
She armed þeos philosophres oolde •
Of worldely thing / þat þey nougħt tolde •
Recorde vponn Dyogenes •
On Plato / and on Socrates •
She made Cypion of Cartage •
To vnderfongen in his aage •
ffor comune proufyte thinges gret •
And for no dreed list not leet •
Ageynst Roome / Þat migħty tovne •
ffor to defende his Regyoun •
Sheo made Hector for his cytee •
To spare for noon aduersytee •
But as a migħty Chaumpyoun •
In þe defence of Troyes toun •
To dye witħ outen feer or dreed •
And þus þis lady who takeþe heed •
Makiþe hir chaumpyouns strong •
Parayllous thinges to vnderfong •
Til þat þey þeyre pourpos fyne •
Recorde of þe worthy nyen •
Of oþer eek þat weere but late •
In1 meene prynces / of latter date •
Herry þe fyffe I dare sey soo •
He migħt beo tolde for oone of þoo •
Empryses wheeche þat were bygonne •
He lefft not til þey weere wonne •
And I suppose / and yowe list see •
Þat þees ladyes alle three •
Wer of his counseyle doutelesse •
fforce Prudence / and Rigħtwysnesse •
Of þeos three / he tooke his Roote •
To putte fortune vnder foote •
And sitħ þis lady / in vertu strong •
Soousteneþe trouthe and dooþe not2 •
Late hir nowe to more and lasse •
.i. republica. •
Notes
  1. The EETS edition has "I" here but both EETS Additional 29729 and this witness "In" here. This is one of the few places where MacCraken notes he has made a correction in the notes of his edition. ↩
