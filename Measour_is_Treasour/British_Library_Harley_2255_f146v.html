Among boorys / beevys and leounys •
Myn office is to walke in wyldnesse •
Reste anyght /in cavys and dongeounys •
Tyl phebus shewe / amorwen his bryghtnesse •
Now stonde I here / to kepe in sekirnesse •
This hows in sewyrte / with al my besy cure •
to letyn in folk / that of gentilnesse •
lyst hem governe / Iustly be mesure •
Explicit quod lydgate •
