Folio 181 Recto
Compare Witnesses: •
With Maria called Maudeleyne •
erly eche morowe / while that my lyfe may dure •
fro slouthe and sombre / I shall my selff restreyne •
to seke Ihesu / at his sepulture •
whome for to fynde / yf that I may recoure •
to have pssession1 / of hym at liberte •
ther were in erthe / no richer creature •
to whome ech whight / bowe shall his kne. •
In mercyfull Ihesu / to putte a verray preffe •
of his mercy / that to mann disepayre •
vppon the crosse / gaf graunt onto the theffe •
to paradys / with hym to repayre •
toke out of helle / soules many A payre •
mangre cerberus / and all his creuelte •
O gracious Ihesu / benygne and debonayre •
have mercy on all / that bowe to the ther kne. •
The name of Ihesu / swettest of names all •
geyn ghostly venymes / holsomest triacle •
for whosoevar / vnto this name calle •
of cankered surfetes / fynt reles by myracle •
to eyen blynd / light lanterne and spectacle •
and bryghtest merrour / of all felicite •
support and sheld / defence and chefe obstacle •
to all that knele / to Ihesu on ther kne. •
This royall name / most soverayn of renoun •
this name Ihesu / victorious in batayle •
of hevenely trivmphes / the laureat guerdoun •
The spirituall palme / of gostly apparayle •
Celestiall prowesse / whiche may most avayle •
to syt with angeles / in ther eternall se •
The Imperiall conquest / nat get with plate or mayle •
but withe meke knelyng / to Iesu on owr kne. •
Notes
  1. The scribe appears to have omitted the "o" here. ↩
