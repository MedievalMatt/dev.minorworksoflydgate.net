Folio 182 Recto
Compare Witnesses: •
At welles fyve / licour I shall drawe •
to wasshe the rust / of my synn a[e]s blyve •
wher all mysteries / of the old and nowe1 lawe •
toke origynall / moraly to descryve •
I mene the welles / of cristes woundes fyve •
wherby we cleyme / of mercyfull piete •
thurgh helpe of Ihesu / at gracious port taryve •
ther to have marcy2 / knelyng on owr kne •
I in Ihesu / sette for Iocunditas •
gynnyng and ground / of all gostly gladnesse •
E next in ordre / is eternytas •
tokene and signe / of eternall brightnesse •
S sette for sanatas / socour ageynn •
V for vbertas / of spirituall plente •
S for sanatas3 / from whome cometh all swetnesse •
to them that knele / to Jhesu on ther kne. •
I in Ihesu is Ioye / that nevar shall ende •
E signyfieth / evarlastyng suffisaunce •
S owr savacioun / whann we shall hens wende •
V his fyve woundes / that made vs acquyntaunce •
from sathanes myght / thurgh his meke suffraunce •
S for the sacrament / which eche day we may se •
in forme of brede / to save vs fro myschaunce •
whann we devoutly / receyve it on owr kne. •
I from Iacob h from habraham •
the lyne descendyng / by generacioun •
C stant for Crist / that from heuene kam •
borne of A mayde / for owr redempcion •
The sharpe / titel4 tokene of his passioun •
whan he was nayled / cpon the rode tre •
O blissed Ihesu / do remissioun •
to all that aske mercy on ther kne. •
Notes
  1. Other witnesses and the standard edition have "newe" here, but the form of the glyph is too similar to "moraly," "toke," and "woundes" in the same stanza to assume this is a malformed "e," especially when compared with the scribe's e forms throughout. See the "e" at the end of this word, "lawe," and "the" in this line and "mysteries", "welles," and "cristes" elsewhere in the stanza. ↩
  2. Again, this appears to be an "a" where other witnesses would have "e" or a suspension for "er." "Marci" for "merci" is known, however (see the Middle English Dictionary entry for examples), so there's no need to presume in this case that it's scribal error. ↩
  3. This appears to be a scribal error occuring in both this word and "sanatas" in line five, perhaps caused by the similarity between "sanitas" and "sauauitas" if one is not careful and further facilitated by the lack of an "i" in "vbertas" above. ↩
  4. The word "titel" is bracketed here on the right side and bottom. ↩
