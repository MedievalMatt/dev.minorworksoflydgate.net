Folio 181 Verso
Compare Witnesses: •
patriarkes / and prophetes one by one •
thre Ierarchies / and all thordres nyne •
twelve apostles / and martirs everychone •
holy confessours / and every pure virgyne •
to blissedd Ihesu / most mekely shall enclyne •
ffowles bests / and fysshes of the se •
kynde hath taught them / by naturall disciplyne •
mekely to Ihesu / to bowe a doun ther kne. •
Ther is no love / parfitely I grounded •
but it of Ihesu / toke his origiynall •
for vpon Ihesu / all parfitenesse is founded •
Owr towr owr castill / geyn powrs infernall •
Owr portecolys / owr bolewerk and owr wall •
Owr sheld owr pavys / geyn all adversite •
Owr heritage / owr guerdoun etarnall •
to whome all creatures / bowe shall ther kne. •
Condygne lawde / nor commendacioun •
yove to his name / ther can no tonge telle •
Of gostly fode / richest refectioun •
hede spryng of grace / of lyff conduyt and welle •
Jhesu namyd / ther dar no dragoun dwelle •
blissedest bawme / of owr felicite •
all cancred sores / and poysouns to repelle •
from them to Ihesu / that knelle vpon ther kne •
This name Ihesu / by interpritacioun •
is for to sayne / owr blissed savyour •
Owr stronge sampson / that strangled the lyon •
Owr lord owr maker / and owr creatowr •
And by his passioun / fro deth owr redemptiour •
Owr orpheus / that from captyvite •
ffett Erudice / to his celestiall towr •
to whome all creatures / bowe shall ther kne. •
